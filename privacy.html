Information We Collect

The App may collect anonymous analytics data through Apple's default analytics services, and this information is only used to aid in future product improvements. Users can opt out of this data collection at any time by the following steps:

On iPad:

  1. Opening the Settings app.
  2. Navigate to Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements.
  3. Turn off Share iPhone Analytics.

On Mac:

  1. Opening the System Settings app.
  2. Navigate to Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements.
  3. Turn off Share with app developers.
  4. Optionally also turn off Share Mac Analytics.

The exact settings may be different depending on the version of operating system on each platform.

Information Usage

The App does not transmit files or mapping data created by the User to any external sources, meaning the files and data created stay on the User's devices, with the exception that the User may explicitly choose to save, copy, or duplicate the files created onto iCloud Drive® and other cloud-based storage services.
